What We Do
Our Services

We provide end-to-end construction and maintenance services within MES jurisdiction, executed by skilled labour and supervised by qualified engineers.

🏗

Civil Construction

New construction of barracks, offices, boundary walls, roads, and allied defence infrastructure as per MES specifications and BOQ.

⚡

Electrical Works

Internal and external electrification, wiring, sub-station works, and allied electrical installations in defence premises.

🔧

Maintenance & Repair

Annual maintenance contracts (AMC), whitewashing, plumbing, sanitary works, and periodic repairs of defence buildings.

🛣

Roads & Drainage

Construction and repair of internal roads, pathways, storm water drains, and allied works within cantonment areas.

🏠

Residential Works

Renovation and upkeep of family quarters, officers' mess, and other residential establishments under MES jurisdiction.

📐

Specialised Works

RCC works, flooring, false ceiling, painting, and other finishing trades as per BOQ and MES-approved drawings.
